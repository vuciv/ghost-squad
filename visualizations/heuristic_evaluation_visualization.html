Understanding Heuristics

A heuristic is a rule of thumb that helps the AI make quick decisions without perfect information. Each heuristic evaluates one aspect of the game state and returns a score.

The 8 Core Heuristics

Heuristic Purpose Formula Default Weight
Ghost Danger Avoid non-frightened ghosts -2500 / (distance + 1) -2500
Choke Point Danger Avoid ghost-controlled intersections Sum of -800 / (dist_to_intersection + 1) -800
Positional Advantage Prefer open spaces (escape routes) reachable_tiles × 80 +80
Frightened Ghost Bonus Chase vulnerable ghosts +1200 / (distance + 1) +1200
Power Pellet Urgency Grab power pellets when in danger +6000 / (ghost_distance + 1) if on pellet +6000
Progress Score Reward eating dots dots_eaten × 200 +200
Distance to Food Move toward nearest pellet -3 × min_distance -3
Exploration Bonus Keep moving in food deserts +150 if no nearby pellets +150

How Weights Work

Each heuristic's output is multiplied by its weight before being summed into the final score: 

final_score = (ghost_danger × weight₁) +
              (choke_point_danger × weight₂) +
              (positional_advantage × weight₃) +
              ... (all 8 heuristics)

Why These Specific Values?

  • Ghost Danger (-2500): Extremely high to ensure survival is top priority
  • Power Pellet Urgency (+6000): Even higher - life-saving move
  • Frightened Bonus (+1200): Strong incentive to hunt when safe
  • Progress (+200): Moderate reward for game progress
  • Distance Penalty (-3): Gentle pull, doesn't override safety

Defensive vs Aggressive Strategies

By adjusting weights, you can change Pac-Man's personality:

  • Defensive: Increase Ghost Danger and Positional Advantage weights
  • Aggressive: Increase Progress and decrease Ghost Danger weights
  • Greedy: Maximize Distance to Food penalty
  • Hunter: Maximize Frightened Ghost Bonus

Real-Time Adaptation

The AI evaluates every possible move direction and picks the one with the highest score. This happens 10+ times per second, allowing Pac-Man to react instantly to changing conditions.
